About Me

I am a Web Developer based in Orange County. Growing up I developed a passion for problem solving and exploring new ideas. While studying architecture, I quickly found myself learning python to optimize my work flow in both creating and documenting geometry for designers and fabricators.

Figuring out the patterns and logic needed to make my code function quickly became something fun and enjoyable. Seeing my code grow and develop into something visual or physical was not only exciting but also gave me a sense of fulfillment. I eventually realized I needed to realign my interest towards web development to allow myself the opportunity to refine my skills and do something I really like.

When i'm not programming you can find me building stuff at home with my CNC mill or 3D printer, but even then I might be programming my machines to help me do the job.
